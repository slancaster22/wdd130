Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I make a reservation?

A: To make a reservation, please visit our Contact Us page and fill out the reservation form. Our team will get back to you shortly.

Q: What should I bring for a rafting trip?

A: We recommend bringing sunscreen, a hat, sunglasses, a water bottle, and a change of clothes. Wetsuits and other equipment will be provided by Dry Oar.

Q: Are children allowed on rafting trips?

A: Yes, children aged 8 and above are welcome on our rafting trips. However, there are certain age and weight restrictions for safety reasons.

Q: Can I bring my own equipment?

A: Yes, you can bring your own equipment if you prefer. However, please ensure that it meets safety standards and regulations.

Q: What is the maximum group size for rafting trips?

A: The maximum group size for our rafting trips is 10 participants. For larger groups, please contact us for special arrangements.
